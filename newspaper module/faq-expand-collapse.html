Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your registered email ID and follow the instructions sent to your inbox to reset your password.

Can I log in using my mobile number?

Currently, login is supported only through your registered email address. Please use the email ID you provided during subscription to log in to your account.

How can I log in to my existing account?

Go to the login page and enter your registered email address and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password?" option to reset it.

How will happen after a month for Monthly Subscription

Your monthly subscription will automatically renew after 30 days unless you cancel it before the renewal date. You'll be charged the same amount for the next month's access.

Is login required every time I visit the ePaper site?

No, once you log in successfully, your session will be remembered for a certain period. However, for security reasons, you may need to log in again after extended periods of inactivity.

How do I log in to the Deccan Herald ePaper?

Visit the Deccan Herald ePaper website, click on the login button, and enter your subscription credentials (email and password). If you don't have an account, you'll need to subscribe first.
